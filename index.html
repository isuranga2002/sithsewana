Our Story

A Home Built with Love

Sith Sewana was founded on 8th May 1999 by Mr. Sarath Lanka Perera in the serene setting of Tanamalwilla, Sri Lanka. Starting with just five children, his vision was simple yet powerful — to create a space where differently-abled children could discover their hidden potential and become valued members of society.

Over the years, the home grew to accommodate 70 children, with dedicated dormitories for boys and girls, a dining hall, and spaces for religious worship. After Mr. Perera's passing in December 2022, his daughters Christeen and Therisita Perera carry forward his legacy with the same devotion and care.

95% of the children come from low-income families who rely on chena cultivation for survival. Sith Sewana gives these children a chance at a better life.

What We Do

Our Programs

We provide comprehensive care and development programs tailored to each child's unique abilities and needs.

Residential Care

Safe and nurturing living environment with separate dormitories for boys and girls, a dining hall, and all essential facilities for daily life and comfort.

Therapeutic Clinics

Specialized therapeutic programs for both intellectual and physical disabilities, helping children improve motor skills, cognitive abilities, and overall well-being.

Speech Therapy

Regular visits from specially trained speech therapists who work with children to develop communication skills and expressive language abilities.

Vocational Training

Hands-on skills development and craft workshops that equip older children with practical vocations, helping them build independence and self-sufficiency.

Spiritual Well-being

A Buddhist temple and dedicated spaces for Christians and other religions, supporting each child's spiritual growth and personal beliefs.

Education Support

Dedicated support for learning and cognitive development, tailored to each child's pace, helping them achieve academic and personal milestones.
